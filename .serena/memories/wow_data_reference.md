# GuildManager - WoW Data Reference

## Classes

### Classic WoW Classes
- Druid, Hunter, Mage, Paladin, Priest, Rogue, Shaman, Warlock, Warrior

### Expansion Classes
- Death Knight (WotLK+)
- Monk (MoP+)
- Demon Hunter (Legion+)
- Evoker (Dragonflight+)

- Druid: `hsl(28 100% 51%)` - Orange
- Hunter: `hsl(83 52% 65%)` - Green
- Mage: `hsl(194 87% 58%)` - Light Blue
- Paladin: `hsl(334 85% 76%)` - Pink
- Priest: `hsl(0 0% 100%)` - White
- Rogue: `hsl(57 100% 69%)` - Yellow
- Shaman: `hsl(209 100% 44%)` - Blue
- Warlock: `hsl(241 69% 73%)` - Purple
- Warrior: `hsl(30 37% 60%)` - Tan
- Death Knight: `hsl(340 75% 35%)` - Dark Red
- Demon Hunter: `hsl(282 80% 38%)` - Purple
- Monk: `hsl(115 54% 55%)` - Jade Green
- Evoker: `hsl(160 65% 48%)` - Teal

## Roles
- **Tank**: Defense, threat management
- **DPS**: Damage dealing
- **Healer**: Health restoration

## Expansions Supported
- `classic` - Classic/Vanilla WoW
- `tbc` - The Burning Crusade
- `wotlk` - Wrath of the Lich King
- `cata` - Cataclysm
- `retail` - Current retail WoW
